Iron Fist

Iron Fist, a Marvel Comics superhero, is the alias of Danny Rand. He gains incredible martial arts skills and the ability to channel a mystical energy called the "Iron Fist" after training in the mystical city of K'un-Lun. With his superhuman strength and glowing fist, he fights to protect the innocent. Danny also joins forces with heroes like Luke Cage and Daredevil, becoming part of the Heroes for Hire team. Known for his discipline and inner strength, Iron Fist is both a skilled warrior and a protector of justice.
